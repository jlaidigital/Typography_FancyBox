Fishing By Moon Phase!

This API will tell you if today is a good fishing day, calculated based on the moon phase. It will provide the best fishing time for both during the day and night for your location.

Details on weather and wind speed are also available if you are fishing on a boat.

Read More

Some anglers believe the moon can impact fishing outcome. This application will rate your chance of catching fish with a score out of 5 depending on the moon phase for the night.

Best fishing time are proven to be 45 minutes before and after sunrise, and 45 minutes before and after sunset.
